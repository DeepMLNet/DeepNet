BlasTransposedMatrixBatchTmpl

Namespace: SymTensor.Compiler.Cuda
Parent Module: ArgTemplates

BLAS view of ArrayND. The ArrayND is implicitly transposed and exposed as a "(float *)[]". All but the last two dimensions are exposed as batches.
